Welcome to Listen4Insight

Here you can find a compilation of content with leadership-related themes created by the Lockheed Martin Leadership Institute at Miami University. The Lockheed Martin Leadership Institute was established to address the need for a new kind of transformational leadership development for a new kind of world. The rigorous curriculum of six classes over three years is designed to challenge the students to step out of their comfort zone and think strategically, collaborate successfully with others, and effectively communicate ideas. The goal of Listen4Insight is to cultivate and share the ideals of transformational leadership, creativity and innovation, and qualities that are essential for a societally-engaged leader. These themes are discussed between leaders such as: engineering and computing students, recent graduates, and industry professionals. We invite you to take a listen.
